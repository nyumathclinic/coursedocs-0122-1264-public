    Before each lecture, you will be expected to complete a reading assignment. The assignment may also include watching videos and completing a basic quiz.     Problem sets are due at the end of each week. They are designed to challenge you to apply the concepts into new or unique situations. Problem sets are also where you learn to present worked solutions clearly and logically.

    Exams.

    There will be three chapter tests (70 minutes long, during lecture), and one final exam (100 minutes long, during finals period). Chapter tests are summative assessments and comprehensive of the course material taught until that point. Exams are designed to test your mastery of the course material at a deep and synthetic level, and whether you can apply it to new settings. The final will be cumulative.
